How is Australian Wine Comparing to the World?

If you are fan of wine, Australia is a perfect place for you!

Australia is the 8th country in the world in wine producing with 4957 different wines, and located in the 8th grade in a list of the best wines with an average country rate of 87.89 (from a scale of 80 to 100).
